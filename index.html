A shared camera app for capturing moments together.

Moment is a social camera app where the only way to create a post is for two friends to be physically together. One person starts a session, nearby friends join over peer-to-peer, and when the shutter fires, every device captures at the same time. The result is a multi-perspective photo set that's impossible to fake — you had to be there.
