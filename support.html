Common Questions

How do I create a Moment?

Tap the camera tab, then "Start Moment." Nearby friends can join your session, and when you tap the shutter, everyone captures at the same time.

How do I add friends?

Friends are added by taking a Moment together. Start or join the same Moment session with someone nearby, and the app adds you as friends automatically.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account." This removes your account, profile, uploaded photos, and friend connections from our systems.

Do I need internet to take photos?

No. Photo capture and transfer happen over peer-to-peer — you just need to be near your friends. Internet is only needed to sync Moments to the shared feed.
